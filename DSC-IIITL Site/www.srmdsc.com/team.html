About the team

DSCSRM aspires to expand its student community of passionate and fervent developers to encapsulate the latest and advanced technologies such as cloud computing, machine learning, big data, web, and app development, etc. through their interactive and free workshops. The students get an exceptional experience through the seminars conducted involving the industry professionals. With the cooperation of various domains such as Technical, Business, Creatives and Public Relations and Documentation these events are organized with rewarding feedbacks.
